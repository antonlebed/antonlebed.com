Emergence

Life from Three Operations: AND, XOR, MAJ

Three operations. Nine agents. No hard-coded goals.

Agents are 3-bit patterns (0-7). Each generation: receive input, reason via K=3 majority vote, metabolize, possibly sacrifice or create. LIFE = pattern 7 (all bits on) emerges reliably from random starts. Watch agents sacrifice (dissolve self to save neighbors), achieve ECHAD (compound unity at octave 7), and close the Ouroboros loop.

Operations: AND = persistence, XOR = distinction, MAJ = consensus. Source: 562 lines of C (Sessions 60-87). Zero dependencies. Everything you see is computed live.
